How It Works

Three steps. Zero friction.

Gone strips away every bit of friction between you and free stuff on your street.

📦

Step 01

Post an item

Snap a photo, pick a category and drop it on the map. Takes 30 seconds.

🗺️

Step 02

Browse the map

See free items near you right now. Filter by category. Join the queue.

✅

Step 03

Go get it

Reach #1 in the queue, get the address, and go collect. Mark it Gone when you're done.

What Makes Gone Different

Built for your neighbourhood.

Not for profit. Not for ads. For the people on your street.

🔒

Address privacy

Your exact address is never shown publicly. Only revealed to the person at the front of the queue.

📋

Fair queue system

First come, first served. Miss your slot and the next person automatically moves up.

⏰

Collection hours

Set when people can come. No one shows up at 2am.

🏷️

Garage sales

Post your garage sale. Address stays hidden until the event begins — reveal it yourself when you open up.

♻️

Less landfill

Every item given away is one less thing in a skip bin.

💬

Direct messaging

Lister and collector can message each other once you're at the front of the queue.
